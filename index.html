Transforming Rural Education

Bridging the educational gap in rural Andhra Pradesh through innovative learning programs, skill development initiatives, and community-centered training approaches.

Our Vision

To create an educated and skilled rural India where every individual has access to quality learning opportunities, enabling them to build prosperous and sustainable livelihoods while preserving their cultural heritage.

Our Mission

To democratize education and skill development in rural communities through innovative training programs, digital literacy initiatives, and vocational courses that align with modern economic opportunities while respecting traditional knowledge systems.

Empowering Rural India Through Education

Sanjeevini Educational and Econimic Development Society (SEEDS) is a registered nonprofit organization (Society Registration Act - Regd. No. 60/2021) dedicated to transforming rural education in Andhra Pradesh. We believe that education and skill development are the cornerstones of rural prosperity and social progress.
